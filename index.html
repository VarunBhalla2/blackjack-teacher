Quick Rules

  • Goal: Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over
  • Card Values: 2-10 = face value, J/Q/K = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Blackjack: Ace + 10-value card = instant win (unless dealer also has blackjack)
  • Dealer must hit on 16 and below, stand on 17 and above
  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Take one more card and stand
  • Split: Split matching cards into two hands
